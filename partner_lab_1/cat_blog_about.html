All About Ashton

Ashton is a 6-year-old doll-faced Persian cat. He loves napping, laying in the sun, christmas trees, chicken, and tuna. But most importantly, he loves peace and quiet.

Some of his dislikes include loud noises, loud people, and loudness.
However, he will tolerate noise, if and only if, it makes for great gossip for his blog.
