A Cat Blog by a Very Fancy Kitty

Dear readers,

Today, Layla barked in my presence.

I tried to tell her that this was strictly prohibited under the law. However, she persisted. She later claimed there was an intruder. I had never heard such a ridiculous thing.

She almost scared away all the birds. I would have had to reschedule my bird watching hour and it would have thrown off the whole day.

Luckily, I kept my cool. As I always do because I am a very fancy cat.

However, due to this whole ordeal, I only get 14 hours of sleep. I have never been so sleep-deprived in my life. I must hibernate for the next 3 days to make up for this. A cat needs their beauty sleep, or else they will get hairballs.

And I do not want to deal with that.

Especially so close to my birthday.

My need to be well-rested and feeling my best for my big, yet very very quiet, 7th birthday party.

It's a surprise party that I'm throwing for myself. I invited Layla and hopefully she reads the dress code.

No muddy paws allowed!
Happy early birthday to me!

Sincerely, Ashton
